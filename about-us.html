About Dragon & Associates

Dragon & Associates (D&A) is a law firm established in 2018 with the goal of becoming one of the leading reputable legal consulting firms in Vietnam.

We operate under the guiding principle of "professionalism – efficiency – reliability," providing high-quality legal services at reasonable costs, and supporting the development of both domestic and international businesses.

D&A specializes in providing the following legal services:

  • Corporate and Investment Consulting
  • Commercial, Labor, and Corporate Governance Consulting
  • Real Estate, Construction, and Planning Law
  • Litigation at Courts and International Arbitration

We are proud to have been and continue to be a partner to many domestic and international clients, contributing to a transparent legal environment and sustainable development in Vietnam.
